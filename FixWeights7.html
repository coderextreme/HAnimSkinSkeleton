X3D Rigging: Auto-Fitter & Line-of-Sight Culler

Select your X3D file. This script will:

  1. Wipe out all existing rig data to ensure a clean slate.
  2. [NEW] Verify if the skeleton is inside the skin. If a joint is outside the mesh, it will be automatically repositioned to the volumetric center of its assigned skin.
  3. Perform 3D Raycasting to cull occluded weights.
  4. Preserve blocked weights ONLY if deleting them leaves a vertex orphaned.
  5. Normalize standard joints to 1.0, assign all vertices to humanoid_root at 1.0, and output the file.
