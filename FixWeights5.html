X3D Rigging: Line-of-Sight Auto-Culler

Select your X3D file. This script tracks the relationship between joint centers and skin vertices. If the linear path from a Joint Center to a Vertex intersects the skin mesh, the weight is flagged.

Blocked weights are removed, unless removing them would leave the vertex with no standard joints at all (in which case the rule is broken and the strongest blocked joint is kept). Remaining weights are normalized to 1.0, and humanoid_root receives all vertices at 1.0.
